Algebric Formulas

  • (a+b)2= a2+b2+2ab
  • (a-b)2= a2+b2-2ab
  • (a-b)(a+b)= a2-b2
  • (a+b)3= a3+3ab2+3ba2+b3
  • (a-b)3= a3-3ab2+3ba2-b3
  • a3+b3= (a+b)(a 2-ab+b2)
  • a3-b3= (a-b)(a 2+ab+b2)
  • (a+b+c)2= a2+b2+c2+2(ab+bc+ac)
  • a3+b3+c3-3abc = (a+b+c)(a2+b2+c2-ab-bc-ca)
